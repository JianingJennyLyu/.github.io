How To Help





Wondering what you can do to help this global issue?



Reduce Your Use of Single-Use Plastics

Try to stop using single-use plastic. Some commonly used single-use plastics are straws, take-out container, take-out utensiles, bottles, bags, etc. When you refuse single-use plastic items, help businesses by letting them know that you would like them to offer alternatives.

Participate In Cleanup Organizations

Many non-profit organizations offer trash pickup and plastic pickup at local beaches and parks.

Support Organizations

It takes a lot of money to clean up trash and help animals injured by plastic. Almost all organizations out there accept donations to help them fininancially so can continue there work. Every small amount counts!

Spread the Word

Inform people on this issue. Plentiful of people around the world still is unaware of this problem. Tell this issue to your family and friend so they can also help and be a part of this!

Recycle and Throw Away Trash

Obviously, in order to prevent plastic to be in places it does not belong is to recycle plastic in the right place. In different states, plastic bottles and cans with labels can also be exchanged for money when recycled to local govenerment recycling areas and machines.
