What's So Scary About Plastic?

Most people believe that the invention of plastic made human lives easier and better as they don't realize how life-threatening and harmful plastic is. Plastic: Mass-produced and mainly used for bottles, straws, bags, and containers. Plastic is a non-biodegradable object. This means every single piece of plastic produced will never go away. The mass amount of plastic is causing many negative impacts on Earth. Some of the most serious impacts we are facing currently because of plastic are; global warming, land pollution, sea pollution, food chain collapse(especially to marine life), pollutes human water sources, pollutes human food sources, and many more.
